Cell Selection Methods
Single cell Click on any cell
Multiple individual cells Hold Ctrl/Cmd and click on cells
Range selection (drag) Click on a cell and drag to another cell to select a rectangular range
Range selection (Shift) Click on a cell, then hold Shift and click on another cell to select all cells in between
Right-Click Right click on cell to show Edit Cell
Class Names
accordion Creates an Accordion header for a panel
accordion-header Element Type: Row - Row becomes accordion until closest or next accordion-header
sp-$index sp- of any index of a column creates a toggle for the column
Mobile Style New Cell
highlighted Element Type: Cell - turns a table cell purple (add-gray to highlighted to change color to gray)
sideways-face Element Type: Cell - Flips the text horizontal
freeze-pane Element Type: Column/Row - Freezes a row or a Column
