Cell Selection Methods:
  1. Single cell: Click on any cell
  2. Multiple individual cells: Hold Ctrl/Cmd and click on cells
  3. Range selection (drag): Click on a cell and drag to another cell to select a rectangular range
  4. Range selection (Shift): Click on a cell, then hold Shift and click on another cell to select all cells in between
  5. Right-Click: Right click on cell to show Edit Cell
  6. Double-click Double click to edit a cell
Built-In Class Names:
  1. accordion | Creates an Accordion header for a panel
  2. accordion-header | Element Type: Row - Row becomes accordion until closest or next accordion-header
  3. sp-$index | sp- of any index of a column creates a toggle for the column
Mobile Style
  1. highlighted | Element Type: Cell - turns a table cell purple (add-gray to highlighted to change color to gray)
  2. sideways-face | Element Type: Cell - Flips the text horizontal
  3. freeze-pane | Element Type: Column/Row - Freezes a row or a Column

After selecting cells, you can apply classnames or IDs to them.
